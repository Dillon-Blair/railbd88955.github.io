Basic Skin Care Tips For Hair Removal

  1. Preparation

    • When shaving, always do so after or during the shower so that your face is warmed up for a razor blade.

  2. Products

    • Shaving Cream: use a gentle cream that is not too foamy.

    • After Shave: use a non-alchoholic after shave to avoid dryness and irritation of the skin.

    • Razor: use an electric razor. If you cannot use an electric, use a high quality one blade(+) depending on your needs.

  3. Post-Shave

    • After shaving, first rub an ice cube across the shaved area, then apply the after shave. You are done!
