Basic Skin Care Tips & Tricks

  1. Cleanse

    • To start, you want to use a facial cleanser everyday in the shower.

  2. Moisturize

    • After you cleanse, you want to apply a facial moisturizer to avoid drying out your skin.

  3. Sunscreen

    • After you moisturize, you want to apply sunscreen with an SPF 30 or higher to protect you skin from photodamage.
