Dry Skin, Oily Skin

Basic Skin Care For Dry/Oily Skin Types

  1. Cleanser

    • Dry Skin: use a gentle, non-abrasive cleanser.

    • Oily Skin: use a stronger cleanser to get more oil off.

  2. Moisturizer

    • Dry Skin: use a moisturizer meant for sensitive skin that has ceramides

    • Oily Skin: use a light moisturizer that is noncomedogenic.

  3. Sunscreen

    • Dry Skin: use a sunscreen with an SPF of 30+. Use topical sunscreen

    • Oily Skin: use a sunscreen with an SPF of 30+ and is noncomedogenci. Use topical sunscree.
